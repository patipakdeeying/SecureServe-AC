About SecureServe

SecureServe is a comprehensive and advanced anti-cheat solution for FiveM, designed to keep your server secure and ensure a fair gameplay environment. Completely free and open-source, SecureServe aims to provide top-notch protection against malicious scripts, exploits, and unauthorized triggers.

Announcement: We plan to introduce an Web Config Panel if we reach 50 GitHub stars! Help us grow by starring the project and encouraging others to do the same.

Why Choose SecureServe?

Robust Protection

Detects and prevents unauthorized events, resources, and entities — ensuring your server’s integrity remains intact.

Configurable Whitelisting

Whitelist your safe events to avoid false bans and maintain a flexible environment that fits your server’s unique needs.
